Waqaf Dana Niaga

Waqaf Dana Niaga is an initiative to provide zero-interest capital (Al-Qardhul Hasan) to small-scale entrepreneurs to start or grow their businesses. The initiative is aimed at promoting Islamic finance and drive the economic growth of Muslim entrepreneurs. As at December 2017, a total of RM 879,300 has been distributed to 392 recipients.

Waqaf Briged

Briged Waqaf is a voluntary-based group of which members are the employees of Johor Corporation and its Group of companies. They are actively involved in providing humanitarian aids and conducting social programmes. In 2015, three Humanitarian Aid Missions were launched whereby members of Briged Waqaf were deployed to help the victims of flood disaster in the East Coast of Peninsular Malaysia. In order to uplift the competency of its members, a three-day Disaster Relief Management and Emergency Aid training was conducted by Kompeni Khalid Al-Walid on 9 May 2015 at Tunjuk Laut Beach Resort, Tanjung Leman, Mersing. In 2017, another company of Briged Waqaf was set-up with the name of Muhammad Al-Fateh, led by the Local Authority of Pengerang (PBT Pengerang). Briged Waqaf is one of the voluntarily body that helped the flood victims of Pulau Pinang in 2017.

PKP Perbadanan Johor & PKP Induk

PKP Induk is a body that plays vital role in solidifying and coordinating all staff societies within JCorp Group of Companies. Johor Corporation Closed Sports Carnival 2015 was among the activities organised by PKP Induk. Several events were contested including Catur BISTARI and Congkak. Besides, PKP Induk is indeed active in coordinating external sports participations including the ones organised by GEMAPUTRA, ADFIM and Association of Statutory Bodies.

Meanwhile, PKP Perbadanan Johor is a staff society of the headquarters. Johor & Motor Club

Johor Motor Club is among the most active groups under the Automobile Association of Malaysia (AAM). The club organised various motorsport events including Johor Clubman Race, Johor Sprint Challenge, Saturday Nite Sprint, Petronas AAM Malaysian Cub Prix Championship and Motorcross Championship. In 2015, Johor Motor Club was entrusted to organise the Motorcross Championship in Bandar Tiram, Johor Bahru. Sponsored by Johor Land Berhad, the biannual event took place on 17 May 2015 and 29 November 2015
