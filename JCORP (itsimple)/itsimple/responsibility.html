Corporate Responsibility

Corporate Responsibility (CR) is the third pillar of Johor Corporation. The duty is upheld by the Corporate Responsibility Division of Johor Corporation, which is operationalised via the CR arms of JCorp that are Waqaf An-Nur Corporation Berhad (WANCorp), Yayasan Johor Corporation, Koperasi Anggota Pekerja Kumpulan Perbadanan Johor Berhad (KOPJCorp) and Intrapreneur Value Creation Sdn Bhd (IVC)

The organizations play strategic roles in carrying out CR activities, including coordinating, monitoring and carrying out the collective charity-based societies under the Amal Business Organisations (ABO), which are patronised by Johor Corporation. There are 25 ABO that are clustered into social development and public welfare; sports and recreation; entrepreneur development; as well as employee welfare.

The CR initiative of Johor Corporation is implemented through various programs that involve all groups of the society at the grassroot level.
